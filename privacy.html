Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

TL;DR: We collect minimal data to provide our service. Your content and data are never sold. You can delete your account anytime. We're GDPR compliant.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ContentOptimizer AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our SEO content optimization service.

This policy complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Data: Email address, name (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Password (hashed), OAuth tokens (Google Sign-In)
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by Razorpay (we never store credit card details)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Content Data: Content you submit for optimization, keywords, SEO scores
  • Usage Statistics: Number of optimizations performed, subscription tier, usage limits
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, referral source

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Authentication sessions, security tokens
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage analytics to improve our service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and maintain our SEO optimization service
  • Process your content and generate SEO recommendations
  • Manage your account and subscriptions
  • Send transactional emails (welcome, usage warnings, receipts)
  • Improve our AI algorithms and service quality
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to provide our service
  • Legitimate Interest: Service improvement, fraud prevention, security
  • Consent: Marketing emails (opt-in only), analytics cookies
  • Legal Obligation: Tax records, fraud prevention, legal compliance

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your data only in these limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication (EU/US data centers)
  • Vercel: Web hosting and serverless functions (global CDN)
  • Razorpay: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Resend: Transactional email delivery
  • OpenAI: AI-powered content analysis (your content is not used for training)

5.2 We Never:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your content with competitors
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share data without your consent (except as legally required)

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Delete your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of marketing emails anytime

To exercise these rights, email us at: privacy@contentoptimizer.ai

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for:

  • Active Accounts: Duration of your subscription + 30 days
  • Deleted Accounts: Immediately deleted (anonymized backups kept for 30 days)
  • Payment Records: 7 years (tax law requirement)
  • Content Submissions: Deleted after 90 days of inactivity

8. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Encryption at rest for database storage
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication available
  • Role-based access control for our team
  • Automated backups with encryption

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and European Union. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Service providers with GDPR-compliant data processing agreements
  • Supabase offers EU data residency options

10. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Cookies Policy

We use the following cookies:

  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in (required)
  • Security Cookies: Prevent fraud and unauthorized access (required)
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how you use our service (optional, consent-based)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling required cookies may affect service functionality.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • In-app notification

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
