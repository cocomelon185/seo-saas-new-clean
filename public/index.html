                How the score works

                Your score combines content depth, on‑page structure, and key tags on a 0–100 scale:

                • Length & depth: Short pages (<500–800 words) get "thin/short" messages. Around 1000+ words is treated as "content length OK, now optimize".

                • Structure: Clear H2/H3 headings, internal links, images with alt text, and schema markup all contribute small positive boosts.
